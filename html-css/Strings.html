Last but certainly not least: The high damage kombo strings. There are not much kombo's, but they're pretty complicated.

  • 1-1-2 cancel into gutted, amplify, Jump punch, f4, db3
    This is the 36% damage kombo. As you can see, you only amplify once, so you'll use one offensive bar from your meter. You don't have to start the combo with 1-1-2, you can use anything, as long as you make sure to cancel into Gutted (bf4). Once you hit you're opponent, they have no way of escaping, exept if they use their brakeaway, wich costs them both of they're defensive meter.

  • 1-1-2 cancel into Gutted, apmlify, 4 1-1-2 gutted
    This is our first corner kombo. When you hit an opponent, they will always move, either a little bit or a lot. But when they reach the conrner, that will not happen anymore. that is what this kombo takes advantage of. The timings are a bit tricky at first, but practice definitly makes perferct. Keep practising, cause this kombo does about 42% damage!
